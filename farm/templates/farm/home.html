Welcome to Summer Wind

Nestled in the heart of the countryside, Summer Wind Farm has been a family-operated business dedicated to providing the finest natural products to our community.

From our sun-ripened blueberries to our carefully crafted homemade salves and premium hay, every product reflects our commitment to quality, sustainability, and traditional farming values.

We take pride in our hands-on approach, ensuring that each product meets the highest standards of excellence while maintaining the natural goodness that only a family farm can provide.
